General Information

podEssence is an AI-powered minimalist podcast player designed for high-density learning. Unlike traditional summary tools that provide only text, podEssence uses AI to identify "high-signal" highlights from long-form episodes and plays them back in the original creator's voice, allowing you to capture the essence of a conversation in minutes.

While most tools provide text bullet points, podEssence focuses on audio-first highlights. It preserves the nuance, emotion, and context of the original recording by curating a "short-form" version of the episode that you can listen to, rather than just read.

Technical & Privacy

We prioritize user privacy. podEssence does not sell your listening habits to third parties. We use your interactions solely to improve the AI's ability to identify high-signal content for your specific interests.

Our models are trained specifically on long-form conversational audio. While AI is highly accurate at identifying key themes and transitions, we also provide a "Context Slider" that allows you to expand the audio around a highlight if you want more detail.

Getting Started

podEssence offers a free tier that includes standard podcast playback and a limited number of AI-generated "Essence" extractions per month. Our Premium tier offers unlimited AI highlights and advanced features for power listeners.

You can access the player directly at www.podessence.app or download the mobile version on the Google Play Store (currently in Beta).
